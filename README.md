   <h1>Go Healthy!</h1>
   <p>Healthy living involves making consistent, daily choices to improve physical and mental well-being, such as eating nutrient-dense foods, engaging in regular physical activity, managing stress, and getting adequate sleep. </p>
   <section>
     <h3>Key Components of a Healthy Lifestyle</h3>
     <ul>
       <li>Nutritious Eating: Focus on a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while limiting saturated fats, trans fats, and added sugars.  </li>
       <li>Sleep & Stress Management: Prioritize 6 to 10 hours of quality sleep per night. Practice mindfulness, meditation, or deep breathing to manage stress, which is crucial for overall well-being.</li>
       <li>Mental & Social Well-being: Maintain strong, supportive relationships and social connections. </li>
     </ul>
   </section>
   <section>
     <h3>Small Steps To Start</h3>
     <p>Building a healthy lifestyle is a gradual process. Start by making small, sustainable changes like taking the stairs, drinking more water, or adding one vegetable to every meal. </p>
